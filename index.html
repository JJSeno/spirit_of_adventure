Arks

Arks are synonymous to teams. Every team will own an ark, and every ark will claim a 64x64 radius area when it is dropped. Every ark gives some sort of bonus to its' tem members. It takes 100 favors to get an ark. There can only be one Ark per type existing a time. That means there are only at most 13 teams allowed atny given moment.

Ark Description

The Ark of the Pig God is generous gives out 4 hunger to all its' believers every sunrise.

The Ark of the Chicken God sustains its flock by |with slow falling and a big jump boost.

The Ark of the Turtle God protects its children by providing 4 golden hearts every sunrise.

The Ark of the Wolf God blesses it's warriors giving additional damage to swords and axes as if they were 2 tiers up.

The Ark of the Ocelot God surrounds it's church with a protective aura, giving them Protection I

The Ark of the Dolphin God teaches its school that with enough effort they can breath and swim like the fishes.

The Ark of the Rabbit God provides it's nest with a speed boost, and a minor jump boost.

The Ark of the Polar Bear God is strong, and so are its' members. Eating uncooked triggers a massive regeneration, cooked meat damages you.

The Ark of the Mooshroom God is poisonus. She gives all it's members a non-lethal poisonous touch.

The Ark of the Sheep God is pious.All it's devouts earn faith and experience every sunrise.

The Ark of the Horse God rewards it's population with the strength to mine with haste.

The Ark of the Parrot God is bored. Somedays it will give you a major luck boost, other days you become unlucky. Holy weapons are a potential reward, death as well

The Ark of the Cow God is known as the God of Hearth. It will provide you 32 logs on every sunrise, provided you have full food.
